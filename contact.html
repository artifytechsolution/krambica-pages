Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KRAMBICA

What are your store hours?

Our stores are open Monday through Saturday from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM (Bhavnagar) and 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM (Vadodara). We're closed on Sundays and major holidays.

Do you offer online shopping?

Yes! You can shop our entire collection online at krambica.com. We offer free shipping on orders over ₹999 and easy returns within 14 days.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of purchase with original tags attached. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Do you offer customization services?

Yes, we offer alterations and customization services at both our store locations. Please visit us or contact us for more details and pricing.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.
