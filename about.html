Our Journey

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming India's trusted fashion destination

2018

The Vision

The idea of KRAMBICA was born. Market research and planning began to understand women's fashion needs and create a unique brand identity focused on quality and affordability.

2019

Foundation & Preparation

Established supplier relationships with premium fabric manufacturers. Built initial inventory and developed the brand's core design philosophy. Created our first collection prototypes.

2020

The Beginning

KRAMBICA was founded in Mumbai with a vision to bring premium quality fashion to every woman. Started with a small team of 5 passionate individuals and a tiny studio space.

2021

First Collection Launch

Launched our first premium cotton kurta collection. Received overwhelming response with 1,000+ orders in the first month. Expanded team to 15 members.

2022

Rapid Growth

Reached 50,000+ happy customers across India. Expanded product range to include Western wear, accessories, and ethnic collections. Opened our first physical showroom in Mumbai.

2023

Sustainable Fashion

Launched eco-friendly and sustainable fashion line. Partnered with local artisans across India. Won "Best Emerging Fashion Brand" award. Team grew to 100+ members.

2024-2025

Going National

Expanded to 5 major cities with physical stores. Launched mobile app for seamless shopping. Reached 200,000+ satisfied customers. Introduced AI-powered personalized styling.
