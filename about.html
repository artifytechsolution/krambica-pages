Our Journey

The KRAMBICA Story

From a small startup to a fashion movement

2020 - The Beginning

Foundation Year

KRAMBICA was born out of a passion for creating affordable, sustainable fashion. Our founders started with a small collection of handcrafted pieces in Mumbai.

2021 - Going Digital

E-commerce Launch

We launched our online platform, making premium fashion accessible to women across India. Our first collection sold out in just 2 weeks!

2022 - Expansion

Growth Phase

Opened our first physical store and partnered with 100+ local artisans. Crossed 50,000 happy customers and launched our sustainable fashion line.

2023 - Recognition

Award-Winning Year

Won 'Best Emerging Fashion Brand' award. Expanded to 5 cities and launched our mobile app with 100K+ downloads.

2024 - Sustainability Focus

Green Initiative

Launched our carbon-neutral collection and committed to 100% sustainable packaging. Reached 200K+ customers nationwide.

2025 - Global Vision

Current

Expanding internationally and launching our sustainable fashion academy to empower the next generation of fashion designers.
